The leave policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees after their probation period is complete.
Full-time employees receive 20 paid annual leave days each calendar year, while part-time employees receive a prorated balance.
Planned leave should be entered in the HR portal at least two weeks in advance so managers can confirm coverage.

Employees may carry forward up to five unused annual leave days into the next calendar year with director approval.
Sick leave should be recorded in the HR portal on the same day whenever possible.
