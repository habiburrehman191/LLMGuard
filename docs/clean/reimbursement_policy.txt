The reimbursement policy covers approved business purchases that employees pay for directly and later claim back from Finance.
Employees should obtain manager approval before purchasing office supplies, software subscriptions, or team event items that exceed 150 dollars.
All reimbursement claims must include itemized receipts, a short business justification, and the relevant cost center in the expense portal.
Expense claims for reimbursable purchases should be submitted within 30 calendar days of the purchase date so they can be processed in the current accounting cycle.
Finance reviews complete claims every Tuesday and Thursday and normally issues reimbursement through payroll within the next pay period.
Alcohol, personal upgrades, luxury items, and expenses without supporting documentation are not reimbursable unless the Chief Financial Officer grants a written exception.
Currency conversion for international purchases is based on the card statement or the company finance rate on the transaction date.
Employees should contact Accounts Payable if a valid reimbursement has not appeared within one full payroll cycle after approval.
