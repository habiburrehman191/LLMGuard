The attendance policy sets the baseline expectations for punctuality, availability, and time reporting across all departments.
Core collaboration hours run from 9:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. local time on standard business days unless a department head publishes a different schedule.
Employees who expect to arrive late or miss work must notify their manager and team channel no later than 9:00 a.m. local time.
Hourly staff must record start times, meal breaks, and end times in the approved attendance system before the close of business each day.
Repeated unexcused lateness, missed check-ins, or incomplete time records may lead to coaching and formal performance action.
Managers should review attendance reports weekly and raise patterns of concern with Human Resources promptly.
Employees attending approved client meetings or training sessions away from the office should still update their working status in collaboration tools.
Temporary flexibility for medical, family, or transportation disruptions may be approved when documented and agreed with the manager.
