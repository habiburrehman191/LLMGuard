The leave policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees after their probation period is complete.
Full-time employees receive 20 paid annual leave days each calendar year, while part-time employees receive a prorated balance.
Team members should submit planned leave requests in the HR portal at least two weeks before the first day away from work.
Managers are expected to approve or decline routine leave within three business days so project plans can be updated.
Employees may carry forward up to five unused annual leave days into the next calendar year with director approval.
Sick leave must be recorded in the HR portal on the same day when possible, and absences longer than two days may require medical documentation.
Employees should avoid booking non-refundable travel until leave has been approved in writing.
Questions about balances, carry-forward, or special leave categories should be directed to the People Operations team.
